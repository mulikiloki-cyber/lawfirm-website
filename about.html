About Us

We are Advocates, Commissioners for Oaths and Notaries Public

E&C is a dynamic law firm combining extensive experience with youthful energy. Our team is led by Collins Ado, Advocate of the High Court of Kenya, with over 20 years of professional experience in litigation, conveyancing, and general legal practice.

With offices in Nairobi and Kajiado, E&C is dedicated to providing fast, reliable, and high-quality legal services to both individual and corporate clients across Kenya and the wider East African region.

Our mission is to provide outstanding, client-centered legal services with integrity, diligence, and compassion.
